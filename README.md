# Grocery-Billing-System_AashishKumar_202501100700002


Problem Statement:
A grocery store wants to calculate the total cost of items purchased by a customer.
- Calculate the total cost of 3 different items.
- Add a 10% discount if the total exceeds $50.
- Display the final payable amount.


Approch
Steps followed in the program:
The user enters the prices of three items.
The program adds all three item prices to calculate the total cost.
A discount is initialized as 0.
If the total cost is greater than $50, a 10% discount is applied.
The final cost is calculated by subtracting the discount from the total cost.
The program displays the billing details:
Total Cost
Discount
Final Cost


Sample Output
Enter the price of item 1: 20
Enter the price of item 2: 25
Enter the price of item 3: 15


---Billing Details---
Total Cost:$60.00
Discount:$6.00
Final Cost:$54.00
